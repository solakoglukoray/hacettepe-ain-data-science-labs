# AIN214 PA2 — Superstore SQL + Netflix EDA

> AIN214 Data Science Lab · Fall 2025

## Overview

Two-part assignment: advanced SQL analysis on a retail superstore dataset, and exploratory data analysis on Netflix content.

## Part 1 — Superstore Sales SQL

Multi-table relational schema built from a US retail dataset.

**Queries include:**
- Top 10 products by total profit with profit margin
- Customer purchasing patterns across segments (Consumer / Corporate / Home Office)
- Sales performance by region and category
- Product pairs frequently bought together (self-join)

## Part 2 — Netflix EDA

Visual exploration of Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset.

- Content type distribution (Movies vs TV Shows)
- Genre trends over time
- Country-based content analysis
- Rating and duration distributions

jupyter notebook ain214_pa2.ipynb
